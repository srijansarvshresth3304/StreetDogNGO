About Us

We are a dedicated team of volunteers and animal lovers working tirelessly to rescue and rehabilitate street dogs across India. Founded in 2023, our NGO has grown to support over 500 dogs with the help of our community.

Our mission is driven by compassion, and we rely on donations and adoptions to continue our work. Learn more about our journey and how you can get involved!
